Brian Chesky
Airbnb
Near-death → Thrived
When COVID killed 80% of revenue overnight, Chesky laid off 25% of staff in one of the most transparent CEO moves on record. Then IPO'd at $100B. Honesty scales.

Stewart Butterfield
Slack (via Glitch)
Pivoted → Thrived
Failed game studio became a $27B company. The lesson isn't "pivot." It's that Butterfield had the self-awareness to recognize the real product hiding inside his failure.
podcastHow I Built This — Butterfield on two failed games and Slack
Reed Hastings
Netflix
Near-death → Thrived
Qwikster was a masterclass in how to lose 800,000 subscribers in a quarter. Hastings reversed it, owned the failure publicly, and rebuilt trust. The apology was the product.
